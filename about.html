Platform story

HVAC projects are easier to compare when the request is organized first.

was built around a simple idea: homeowners should be able to understand their HVAC category, prepare useful project details, and compare independent provider options without confusing the platform with a direct contractor.

The platform focuses on four common HVAC comparison paths: cooling, heating, ductwork and ventilation, and indoor air quality. Each path helps homeowners think through provider fit, quote scope, local availability, licensing, insurance, and warranty questions.

Aggregator model

What the platform does — and what it does not do.

The platform helps organize comparison. It does not perform HVAC services directly.

What it helps with

  • Organizing basic HVAC project details.
  • Comparing independent local provider options.
  • Keeping verification questions visible.

What it does not do

  • It does not install or repair HVAC systems.
  • It does not send technicians or crews.
  • It does not guarantee provider work or pricing.

Why compare first

A better starting point before requesting HVAC quotes.

Many HVAC requests become confusing because the category, scope, provider questions, and verification details are mixed together. A comparison platform keeps those parts easier to review.
